My Story: Growth & Passion for Learning.
About Me

I am a passionate and self-motivated technology professional with a background in Software Development, Data Analytics, Business Analysis, and AI Data Quality Assurance. My professional journey has been driven by continuous learning, adaptability, and a strong desire to solve real-world problems through technology and data-driven decision-making.

I graduated from Kurukshetra University with a Bachelor's degree and later pursued an MBA in Business Analytics from Amity University. This combination of technical and business education has provided me with a unique perspective, enabling me to bridge the gap between technology and business objectives while making informed, data-driven decisions.

My career began in the education sector, where I worked as a Computer Teacher and developed strong communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills. This experience taught me the importance of knowledge sharing, patience, and continuous improvement—qualities that continue to benefit me in my professional career today.

I later transitioned into the IT industry as a Software Developer at Arisen Technologies, where I worked on full-stack web development projects using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL. During my tenure, I contributed to the development of an internal CRM system that improved operational efficiency and participated in website enhancement projects that strengthened my expertise in software development, database management, and business process optimization.

Alongside software development, I developed a strong interest in data analytics and business intelligence. I gained hands-on experience with SQL, Excel, Tableau, and Power BI, building dashboards and analytical solutions that transformed raw data into meaningful insights. Working with data helped me develop a deeper understanding of business performance, customer behavior, and data-driven decision-making.

Currently, I am working as an Associate Analyst at GlobalLogic, where I contribute to large-scale AI and robotics projects. My responsibilities include data validation, annotation review, quality assurance, and quality score evaluation using platforms such as Google Crowd Compute and QASA. Within a short period of joining, I was promoted to the role of Quality Analyst due to my performance, attention to detail, and commitment to maintaining high-quality standards.

My experience in AI data operations has strengthened my analytical thinking, quality assurance expertise, and understanding of how high-quality data contributes to the success of modern AI systems. Working on advanced AI projects has given me valuable exposure to large-scale data operations and emerging technologies that are shaping the future of artificial intelligence and robotics.

Beyond my professional responsibilities, I continuously invest time in learning new technologies and expanding my skill set. My interests include Data Analytics, Business Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, SQL, Python, and AI-driven technologies. I enjoy working on analytical projects that transform complex datasets into actionable insights and meaningful business solutions.

I strongly believe that growth comes from continuous learning, consistency, and a willingness to embrace new challenges. Whether developing applications, analyzing data, building dashboards, or ensuring data quality for AI systems, I strive to deliver high-quality work that creates value and drives positive outcomes.

Thank you for taking the time to learn about my journey. I am excited about future opportunities in Data Analytics, Business Analytics, Data Science, and AI-related domains, where I can continue learning, growing, and contributing to impactful technology solutions.
